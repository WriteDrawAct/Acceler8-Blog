## Top Platforms Non-Technical Leaders Should Know

### 1. Vibecode  
A pioneering “vibe-coding” platform where users prompt an AI using natural language to build iOS apps. It’s already powered over 40,000 app creations—and costs range from free to $200/month. ([Business Insider](https://www.businessinsider.com/vibe-coding-startup-vibecode-helps-build-apps-on-iphone-fundraise-2025-8?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

### 2. Replit (“Vibe Coding”)  
Enabled for rapid prototyping: build an app in an afternoon using AI prompts. Its growth has seen ARR rise from $10M to $100M in just six months. ([Business Insider](https://www.businessinsider.com/replit-ceo-vibe-coding-solo-startups-2025-8?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

### 3. Workato One  
A unified, low-code AI platform that includes:
- **Agent Studio**: Build AI agents without coding,
- **Agent Trust**: Governance and secure deployment,
- **AgentX Apps**: Prebuilt AI workflows.  
Designed to reduce AI fragmentation and streamline operations. ([Axios](https://www.axios.com/sponsored/why-ai-at-the-core-is-key-to-supercharged-enterprise-success?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

### 4. BuildFire AI  
A no-code mobile app builder that generates branded apps from user questions. Great for creating custom, branded mobile experiences in minutes. ([Buildfire](https://buildfire.com/no-code-ai-tools/?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

### 5. Bubble + AI Plugins  
Highly customizable no-code web app builder that integrates AI plugins—excellent for crafting responsive, data-driven applications. ([Litslink](https://litslink.com/blog/no-code-ai-platforms-what-it-means-for-businesses?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

## Implementation Strategy for Non-Technical Leaders

1. **Define the Use Case**  
   Choose pilot projects with high visibility and low complexity—like quick customer tools or workflow automation.

2. **Select the Right Tool**  
   - Use **Vibecode** or **Replit** for app prototypes  
   - Use **Workato One** for enterprise AI workflows  
   - Use **Bubble**, **Zapier**, or **BuildFire** for web/mobile solutions

3. **Establish Governance**  
   Partner with IT to set boundaries, especially around data, permissions, and transferability.

4. **Upskill Citizen Developers**  
   Provide templates, initial training, and champions to ensure success and visibility.

5. **Iterate and Expand**  
   Start small, measure outcomes (time, cost, adoption), and scale with proof of ROI.
